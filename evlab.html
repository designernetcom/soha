Electric Vehicle (EV) Lab Set-Up


In alignment with India’s vision for green mobility, this proposal outlines the establishment of a state-of-the-art Electric Vehicle (EV) Lab in your institution. The initiative aims to provide real-time, industry-ready training to students and faculty, and to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and hands-on implementation.

Electric Vehicle Design & Development

EVRobotics Skill Coach (India) Pvt. Ltd. brings over a decade of experience in electric vehicle training, certification, and industry-aligned lab setups across India. We offer a modular lab solution, compatible with AICTE funding and self-funding programs like:

  • MODROB – Modernization and Removal of Obsolescence
  • RPS – Research Promotion Scheme
